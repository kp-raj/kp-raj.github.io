Guess Lyrics

Tech stack used: Flask, Python, Openpyxl(Excel), HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript

Like all other music loves, I love listening to songs. Songs with good lyrics make them even better.

I decided to build an application where user is randomly given few lines from a song and must guess the song, movie, or lyricist. I started a habit of noting down great lyrics from songs I listen to and created an excel sheet with Telegu and Hindi movie songs.

I used Flask framework and Pythonâ€™s Openpyxl library to interact with the excel sheet and randomly picked rows to display. Random multiple choices are given to the user and upon choosing correct lyricist, they are awarded double points.

The app also saves the points the user has earned and maintains a leaderboard. I deployed the app using Heroku.
