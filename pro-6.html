Bills

People usually stop caring about the policy making once they choose their representatives. I believe that people should be aware of what policies are being discussed and what is their representative’s stand on each of those bills.

My experience, as an intern at the Lok Sabha of the Parliament of India, has made me realize the epic process of policy making. I collected every bill that had been introduced in both the houses of Parliament from 1947 to 2020 and saved the information in a database.

I created a web application where a user can search for any bill that has been introduced, passed, assented, negatived, pending, withdrawn with various types of filters. I deployed the app using Heroku.


See it Live

Extensions planned: Use the Right To Information Act (RTI) to collect the data of the votes of every representative regarding each bill. Display those votes of every representative/party to know what their stand. Showing who introduced what bill and who voted against or towards it.
