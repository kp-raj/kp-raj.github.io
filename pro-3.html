Lockdown Lists

Tech stack used: Flask, Python, PostgreSQL, HTML/CSS, Bootstrap, JavaScript

Since March 2020, lockdown has made me try various things from binge watching to cooking. I have always been a movie lover. But staying at home made me watch different types of movies, reads books, watch tv shows that I would never have thought about.

Boredom, internet, and OTTs together broke all language and cultural barriers.

I and my friends started to share our personal favorites from our lockdown watchlist. That gave me an idea to create a web application to collect suggestions of their lockdown lists of great movies, books and shows from people. I used the Flask web framework, Postgresql database and psycopg2 library to build the application.

To restrict multiple duplicate entries from the same person, I started validating email addresses and allowing only 5 entries from each email address. I collect their suggestions using forms and save the data in a database. I, then, display the list with the number of times each entry has been suggested by people.
